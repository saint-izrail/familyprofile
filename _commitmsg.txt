Fix: portal modals to document.body so they aren't trapped by Reveal's will-change

The agenda "Usulkan Momen" dialog (and any modal rendered inside a <Reveal> or
under the backdrop-blur nav) was positioned relative to that ancestor instead of
the viewport — CSS will-change/backdrop-filter/transform establish a containing
block for position:fixed descendants. The overlay no longer covered the screen
and the dialog had no solid backdrop ("macet"). Render SuggestButton, ImageCropper,
PhotoLightbox, and CommandPalette overlays via createPortal(document.body).
